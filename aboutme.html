maharshi

Maharshi (transl.Great sage) is a 2019 Indian Telugu-language action drama film[10] directed by Vamshi Paidipally and produced by Sri Venkateswara Creations, Vyjayanthi Movies, and PVP Cinema.[11] The film stars Mahesh Babu, Pooja Hegde, and Allari Naresh while Jagapathi Babu, Kaikala Satyanarayana, Kota Srinivasa Rao, Prakash Raj, and Jayasudha play supporting roles. The soundtrack and score of the film were composed by Devi Sri Prasad, while K. U. Mohanan and Praveen K. L. handled the cinematography and editing respectively.
