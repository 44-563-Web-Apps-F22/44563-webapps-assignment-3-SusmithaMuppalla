B.M.birla science museum
B. M. Birla Science Museum is an Indian science museum located in Hyderabad, India. Constructed by civil engineer P. A. Singaravelu, it comprises a planetarium, museum, science centre,art gallery as well as a dinosaurium . The museum itself was the second phase of the science centre when it opened in 1990. B M Birla Science Museum is a museum and science center and it also has a planetarium on its premises
